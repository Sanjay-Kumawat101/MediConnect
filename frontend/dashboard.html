🏛️ Government Health Schemes
Ayushman Bharat
Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

Benefits:

  • Free health coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Covers over 1,400 medical procedures
  • Cashless treatment at empaneled hospitals
  • Coverage for pre and post-hospitalization expenses
Eligibility: Based on SECC-2011 database and state-specific criteria
CGHS
Central Government Health Scheme

Benefits:

  • Comprehensive medical care for central government employees
  • Cashless treatment at CGHS hospitals
  • Reimbursement facility for private hospitals
  • Coverage for family members
Eligibility: Central Government employees and pensioners
ESI Scheme
Employee State Insurance

Benefits:

  • Medical care for employees and dependents
  • Cash benefits during sickness and maternity
  • Disability and dependent benefits
  • Funeral expenses coverage
Eligibility: Employees earning up to ₹25,000 per month
Janani Suraksha Yojana
Safe Motherhood Intervention

Benefits:

  • Cash assistance for institutional delivery
  • Free delivery in government hospitals
  • Post-delivery care support
  • Transportation allowance
Eligibility: Pregnant women, especially from BPL families
